Student Management and Evaluation

Education
Client Background

An education provider specializing in competitive exams sought a solution to automate their student management and evaluation processes. Their traditional methods were time-intensive and lacked real-time insights into student performance.

Objectives
  • Simplify and automate student enrollment, attendance, and progress tracking.
  • Reduce manual effort in evaluating assignments and exams.
  • Provide real-time analytics for better decision-making.
Solution Provided

EnlightVision Technologies implemented a tailored digital solution combining AI, automation, and centralized systems to improve operational efficiency and student satisfaction.

Benefits

  • Automated grading of assignments and exams using Computer Vision and NLP.
  • Instant feedback and score reports for students to identify improvement areas.

  • Web and mobile applications to centralize student information, attendance, and academic records.
  • Real-time dashboards for administrators to monitor progress and performance metrics.

  • Designed dynamic, accessible reports for faculty and students.
  • Provided actionable insights and data visualization.
